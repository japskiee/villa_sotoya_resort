Our Story

Villa Sotoya Resort is beautifully situated in Nagcarlan, Laguna. All rooms are spacious with fan or air-conditioned. We offer cottages for those who want to be in tune with nature and have an occupancy of up to 1000 people.

It is definitely a place to spend your vacation and gatherings where you can relax and enjoy peaceful and lush green surroundings.

We have 3 pavilions which can accommodate weddings, debut, birthday parties, small meetings, reunions, catering services and other events with a maximum of 800 pax.
